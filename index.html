About Us

Primotech is an established independent, privately owned company founded in 2014. The company has experience and expertise in weighing solutions, distribution, installation, service, and maintenance of laboratory, scientific, and life science research equipment, and laboratory consumables. Primotech is a full-service providing firm that can help you maximize your return on technology investment (ROI). The company can help you save time by serving as your single point contact for all weighing and laboratory solution services. Primotech's solutions are designed to match the unique needs and requirements of your business.
